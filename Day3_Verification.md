## Reflection

The claim that looked most authoritative but was actually weakest was **Claim 3**: *'The median campus placement package across all engineering streams at IIT Bombay touched ₹20.0 LPA in 2025 (IIT Bombay Annual Placement Report).'* This claim appeared the most credible because it cited an official institutional source — the IIT Bombay Annual Placement Report — with a precise, specific figure, and Perplexity confirmed it with a Yes. But when I opened collegese.com/colleges/iit-bombay-2025/placements, the actual median package did not match the number Gemini stated, making the verdict FALSE. The figure was specific enough to sound trustworthy, which is exactly what made it the most dangerous claim of the five. The lesson: confidence does not equal correctness. The verification step belongs to the human — every time.
